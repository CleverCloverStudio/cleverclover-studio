## PLACEMENT DECISIONS

The video is high-quality and personality-forward, but personality-forward content and homepage-hero content do different jobs. Decisions locked in:

### Homepage Hero — NOT changing (for now)
The current YouTube showreel embed at `public/images/showreel-thumbnail.png` stays. The homepage hero's job is credibility for first-time visitors — casting directors, Eliances members, referred clients — who need to verify in three seconds that this is a real operation. Finished client-grade work does that job. A personality piece, no matter how well produced, is a vibe play and belongs on pages where the visitor has self-selected into wanting to know who Bhodi is.

(A future showreel recut is a separate, later task — not covered by this update.)

### About Page Hero — PRIMARY PLACEMENT
The brand intro video becomes the lead asset on `/about`. The page's existing headline "One studio. Every skill." pairs naturally with a video that walks through every skill across nine settings. This is the page where personality earns its keep, because the visitor is already there to learn about Bhodi.

### YouTube Channel Trailer — SECONDARY PLACEMENT
Set as the channel trailer on the RadiaRadio YouTube channel (https://www.youtube.com/@RadiaRadio) so it autoplays for new visitors. This is the discovery surface where pattern interrupt is the entire point.

### Social Pinned Content — TERTIARY PLACEMENT
Pinned across @bryan7737, @CloverHQ, and @DowntownPhoenixLove as the cornerstone piece. Other Omni content links back to this video as the introduction to the format and the studio.

### Funnel Direction
All placements drive viewers toward CleverClover.studio. The video itself does not need to live ON the homepage to do its job — it needs to send qualified, pre-warmed traffic TO the homepage, where the showreel and the services do the closing.

## GEMINI OMNI CONTENT STRATEGY — THE BIGGER PICTURE

The brand intro video is the cornerstone of an ongoing Gemini Omni avatar content strategy. The full ecosystem (some live, some in development):

### Live or Posted
- **Brand Intro Video** (this document's subject) — the central hub piece all other content links back to

### In Development / Planned
- **"Six Four One Five" / Valley Bar music video setup** — features a lecture hall hook clip; Valley Bar = Charlie Levy connection
- **Hoverboard-on-water comedy clip** — short-form pattern interrupt content
- **Whiteboard Golden Ratio series** — four-part educational series teaching the golden ratio and music theory behind the God Mode Guides. Teased at the end of the brand intro video as a hook into the educational track.
- **Grief-into-music vulnerability series** — planned, longer-form, emotional register

### Strategic Role of Each
- Brand intro video = top of funnel, identity establishment
- Whiteboard series = authority builder, drives toward God Mode Guide ($39) purchases
- Music video setups = portfolio expansion using Omni as production cost-saver
- Comedy / pattern interrupt clips = social reach and discoverability
- Vulnerability series = depth and brand humanization, builds the emotional connection that supports premium pricing

### Common Visual / Production Language
Established by the brand intro video and applied across the ecosystem:
- Bhodi's avatar in unexpected or visually-anchored settings
- Calm, grounded delivery against absurdity or against high-concept visuals
- No background music unless the content requires it — voice and setting ambience first
- 1920x1080 export, edited from 10-second Omni clips
- Five-slot prompt structure with negative prompts and continuity anchors

### Production Workflow (Reusable)
1. Write script in voice
2. Break script into 10-second beat units
3. Write five-slot prompts for each clip (camera → setting → wardrobe → action → line)
4. Add negative prompts and continuity notes
5. Generate via Gemini Omni @bryan7737
6. Edit in Resolve at 1920x1080
7. Export, distribute according to placement strategy
